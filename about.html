This website was created by Toasty the Toaster, a High School programming team from The Dalton School at Hack The Solar System.

Toasty The Toaster consists of William Barkoff, Alex Studer, Claire Wilson, Isha Chirimar, and Ava Mohajer. This website is Open Source and MIT Licensed. Hack The Solar System is the American Museum of Natural History's 5th Annual BridgeUP STEM Hackathon. Toasty the Toaster also participated last year, in Hack The Deep. (See our project last year). This year's challenge, The Women of Space Science, highlights the untold story of a prominent female scientist who has studied physical science, planetary science and astronomy at the Museum.
